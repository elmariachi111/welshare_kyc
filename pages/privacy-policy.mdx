# Privacy Policy

## Data Privacy Disclaimer

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we collect it, and how it is used in connection with our token pre-sale.

## What Data Do We Collect?

For regulatory compliance during the Public Pre-Sale Phase 1, we collect the following data as part of our Know Your Customer (KYC) process: crypto wallet address, email address, data from your ID document and data from your selfie.

## How Do We Collect the Data?

We use Persona Identities Inc., a third-party provider, to collect and verify your data. You can find their data privacy policy here: [https://withpersona.com/legal/privacy-notices](https://withpersona.com/legal/privacy-notices)

## Use of Data

The data we collect is used strictly for the purposes of regulatory compliance and to ensure the security and integrity of the token pre-sale. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as described in this policy.

## Your Consent

By participating in the Public Pre-Sale Phase 1, you consent to our collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

## Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [info@welshare.health](mailto:info@welshare.health).
